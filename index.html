Projects

Bad Debt Prediction

A credit-based company faced financial losses because some high-risk customers were approved for loans and later defaulted on payments. To address this challenge, 100K+ customer credit and behavioral records were analyzed to identify patterns associated with loan default.

A machine learning model was developed to predict bad debt risk before loan approval, enabling risk-aware lending decisions and improving credit evaluation processes.

The model achieved ROC-AUC ≈ 0.74, KS ≈ 34%, and successfully detected around 60% of risky applicants, helping reduce potential credit losses through early risk identification.

Tools: Python, Scikit-learn, Feature Engineering, Model Evaluation

Credit Card Campaign Analysis

A growing bank wanted to expand its credit card business but faced low adoption due to ineffective customer targeting. To address this, transaction and customer data (500K+ records) were analyzed to identify high-potential customer segments.

Exploratory data analysis revealed that the 18–25 age group demonstrated strong spending behavior but had relatively low credit card adoption. Based on this insight, a targeted campaign strategy was designed to improve engagement.

A randomized A/B test was implemented to evaluate the effectiveness of the new campaign. Statistical testing showed a 6.7% increase in average spending and approximately 40% conversion, validating the strategy for broader rollout.

Tools: Python, Pandas, Statistical Testing, Data Analysis

MAP Compliance Monitoring

A brand selling across multiple marketplaces struggled to enforce Minimum Advertised Price (MAP) policies because reseller prices frequently changed and many sellers operated under multiple storefront aliases. This made manual monitoring extremely difficult and inconsistent.

To address this challenge, an automated MAP compliance monitoring system was developed to track more than 150K product listings from 901 sellers across 5 marketplaces. The system continuously analyzes marketplace pricing data to detect violations of MAP and Lowest Price Policy (LPP) rules.

The pipeline consolidates reseller identities, identifies pricing violations, and triggers automated enforcement actions. This significantly reduced manual monitoring work and improved the effectiveness of pricing policy enforcement across marketplaces.

Tools: Python, SQL, Pandas, Data Automation,Report-lab
